Look no further than the SWISSGEAR 3598 backpack to meet your carrying preferences. It’s an everyday backpack that entails modern functionality for commuters and travelers. Its diamond-patterned front panel embeds a pocket with side zippered access, providing extra storage space for your small accessories. The front panel’s top zipper opens into a two-sided organizer panel for your ID cards, credit cards, smartphone, travel documents and other essentials. Unbuckle its adjustable compression straps and find plenty of space for your necessities, plus a padded tablet sleeve within the main compartment. Its construction also offers a side mesh water bottle pocket and a small side accessory pocket. And, to provide ultimate comfort details, its back features a padded mesh airflow panel and plush shoulder straps with mesh breathable fabric.
